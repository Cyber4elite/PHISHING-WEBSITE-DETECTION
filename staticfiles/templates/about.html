Securing the web, one link at a time

PhishShield is a comprehensive phishing detection platform designed to protect users and organizations from malicious URLs through advanced machine learning and heuristic analysis.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves to browse the internet safely. PhishShield was created to provide instant, accurate phishing detection that works seamlessly in the background, protecting users from malicious links before they can cause harm.

Our platform combines cutting-edge machine learning algorithms with proven heuristic techniques to deliver the most accurate phishing detection available, all while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

Technology

PhishShield leverages multiple layers of analysis to detect phishing attempts:

  • Machine Learning: Q-Learning algorithms trained on millions of URLs
  • Heuristic Analysis: Pattern recognition and rule-based detection
  • Reputation Databases: Real-time domain and IP reputation checks
  • Redirect Unfolding: Follow redirect chains to final destinations
  • SSL Analysis: Certificate validation and security assessment
  • Content Analysis: Keyword detection and suspicious pattern matching

Privacy First

No Persistent Storage

We analyze URLs in memory only and never store your data without explicit consent. Your privacy is our top priority.

PhishShield is designed with privacy at its core. We believe that security tools should protect users without compromising their privacy. That's why we:

  • Process URLs in memory only
  • Never store personal data without consent
  • Are fully GDPR compliant
  • Support enterprise SSO and data residency requirements
  • Provide transparent data handling policies
